Recipe #2

  • Time: 30min
  • Serves: Pizza
Ingredients Quantity
2 ½ cups warm water 600mL
2 teaspoons active dry yeast
1 ½ teaspoons kosher salt 3g
¼ cup semolina flour

Step 1:“Bloom” the yeast by sprinkling the sugar and yeast in the warm water. Let sit for 10 minutes, until bubbles form on the surface.

Step 2:In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the middle and add the olive oil and bloomed yeast mixture. Using a spoon, mix until a shaggy dough begins to form.

Step 3:Lightly flour the dough, then cover with a kitchen towel and let rest for another 30 minutes to an hour while you prepare the sauce and any other ingredients.

Recipe #3

  • Time: 3 hours 30 min
  • Serves: Beshbarmak
Ingredients Quantity
1 large onion peeled and sliced into rings divided
2 eggs
plain flour 600 grams
1/2 teaspoon salt
Water to cover

Step 1:Put your meat and 1/2 the onion in a deep enough dish to cover it with cold water leaving enough space on the top so that it doesn't boil over.

Step 2:Bring to the boil, constantly removing the foam, Reduce heat to simmer when it starts to boil. Cover it.

Step 3:Let it cook for about 2 1/2 hours or until the meat is tender and falling off the bone.

Step 3:Meanwhile, prepare your noodle dough.

Step 4:In a bowl mix the sifted flour (300-400 g), the eggs (whisk the eggs before you add into flour), add salt and pour water until a dough is formed.

Step 5:Now cook the noodles in batches in the same stock for about 7-8 minutes. Sieve them out on to the big plate leaving the space in the center for meat. Then add your meat chopped in bite size pieces and put it in the center of the dish. Pour some broth over the meat.
